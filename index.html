Graubünden Mountain Hunt

The Capauliana pictures are historical images of Graubünden, provided by the Fundaziun Capauliana. But how might these places look nowadays? Make yourself ready for this challenge: Pick a picture you like, try to find it on your hike, and take a picture of how the landscape looks nowadays.
